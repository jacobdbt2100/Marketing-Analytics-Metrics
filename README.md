# Marketing Analytics Metrics

Metric	Description	Formula/Example	Note

1. **Ad Spend:**
2. **Revenue:**
3. **Profit:**		Revenue - Ad Spend
4. **Impressions:**	Number of times an ad/post/content was shown (screen displays)
5. **Reach:**	Unique number of users who saw the ad at least once
6. **Awareness:**		impression, view	User saw the ad (top of the funnel)
7. **Engagement:**		like, share, comment, click	User interacted with the ad
8. **Lead/Intent:**		add_to_cart, signup, form_submit	User showed purchase intent but didn't complete it
9. **Conversion:**		purchase, subscribed	User completed the desired business action
10. **Return on Investment (ROI):**	Measures ad profitability rate	(Ads Revenue - Ad Spend) * 100 / Ad Spend
11. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):**		Revenue / Ad Spend
12. **Cost per Click (CPC):**	Measures how much is paid each time a user clicks on an ad in pay-per-click (PPC) campaigns	Ad Spend / Clicks
13. **Cost per Acquisition (CPA):**	Measures how much is paid to acquire one conversion (e.g., purchase, signup, download). A conversion is usually defined by the campaign’s objective	Ad Spend / Conversions
14. **Click-through Rate (CTR):**	Percentage of people who click on a link, ad, or call-to-action after seeing it (engagement rate)	Clicks * 100 / Impressions
15. **Conversion Rate:**		Conversions / Clicks
16. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):**	Total revenue expected from a customer during their entire relationship with the business. Ideally, CLV >> CAC	AOV * PF * CL
17. **Average Order Value (AOV):**		Total Revenue / Total Orders
18. **Purchase Frequency (PF):**		Total Orders / Total Customers
19. **Customer Lifespan (CL):**	Lifespan of a customer (e.g., years, months)	(Max Order date - Min Order date)
20. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**	Average cost a business spends to acquire one new customer	Marketing Spend / New Customers	Components of Costs: Advertising spend (digital ads, TV, print, etc.), Sales team salaries and commissions, Marketing software/tools and technology, Creative & content production costs, Overheads directly tied to customer acquisition
